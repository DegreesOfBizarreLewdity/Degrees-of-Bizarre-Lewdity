Killer Queen

KQ is a Skulduggery skill Stand, leaning towards Medium submissiveness. Submissiveness ranges from 850 to 1150. (This is an estimate)

Overview 

Killer Queen is a Skulduggery skill with med submissiveness Stand. Reasonable strength. Can fight, but doesn't excel at it.

Destructive Power: B |

Speed: B |

Range: E |

Durability: B |

Precision: B |

Ability potential: S |


Abilities

- Bomb Transmutation (4) - Transforms one target into a weak bomb, that implodes and causes severe damage to them.


- Sheer Heart Attack (10) - Creates an automatic bomb that chases your opponent(s). On normal combats, this bomb will find its target at the end of the turn and instantly knock them out. On wrestles, it will distract your opponent, making them waste 1 turn. At the end of the turn, the bomb explodes and deals damage based on how close the opponent (or its Stand) is to it.


- Bites the Dust (6) - Set a remote time bomb that can be activated at your will. For it to be set, you must expose all of your quirks. When activated, detonates time itself and loops back on it, nullifying any effects that happened in that timeframe. Changes made to your body will remain even after the time travel, and only you will have any memory of what happened and what is going to happen. Usable once per day.
