Suddenly, you begin to have a bizarre dream. You're walking on the school hallways. It's very dark, must be past midnight. You see a shadowy, small figure rounding a corner, while holding something. You try to call for them, but your voice doesn't work. You run after, but every step taken seem to apart you even more. Than they look at you, eyes creepily glowing in the darkness, accompanied by a sick smile that cuts through your soul with a sharp pain. You look down. You're bleeding. Something got stuck in your stomach, shining a devilish golden light. But instead of feeling weak by blood loss, the exact opposite happens. You feel stronger than ever.

Upon looking up again, you seem to be somewhere else. At the same time you feel the whole universe around you, and nothingness hugging your flesh. A shining shapeless being appears before you, and begins to talk to you. "Greetings, human. You seem to have acquired enough experience to finally give me a form. You have ignored my presence for so long, so occupied by your mundane activities, and yet you didn't give up. That's why I am here: to guide you in this first step. This is the only way for me to talk to you, and we don't have much time, so I will be brief. The experiences you've acquired by improving your skills can be used to give me ways to better serve you. Not only that, your willingness to be ordered or to control will also determine how I am supposed to act." The spirit opens your mind and shows your available skills, then continues: "You can chose any and how many skills you would like me to absorb. Or, alternatively, you could choose to keep me in this form temporarily. So, what do you choose?" 

Fighting skill: More focused on battle. Brute force.
Swimming skill: Good abilities in combat.
Skullduggery: Not very good at fighting, excellent at dodging the law.
School skills: Do various types of things, depending on what skill you're best at.

"Let me know when you're finished."

(1) I have chosen the skills (2) I prefer to keep you like this


After confirming your stand skills you will be brought to a screen where you may input a stand name.
