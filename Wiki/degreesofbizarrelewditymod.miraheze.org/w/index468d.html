Bastet

Bastet is a sexual skill with high submissiveness Stand. Submissiveness ranges from 1150 to 1500, in other words, a character with the "meek" or "submissive" trait.


Overview 

Sexual skill with high submissiveness Stand. No raw strength. Can protect its user from multiple aggressors.

A stand with only one ability (at the moment) - However, it may be the strongest against large groups next to Heaven's Door, as it can render 2 or more enemies powerless.

Destructive Power: 0 |

Speed: 0 |

Range: 0 |

Durability: 9 |

Precision: 1 |

Ability potential: 10 |


Abilities

Combat Ability: Magnetic Attraction - Turns your (PC) body into a powerful magnetizer that turns everyone who touches you into a living magnet. Only works against 2 or more opponents. Magnetized opponents cannot fight back and retire from battle. Lasts until the end of the combat.


Extra Info

- Bastets ability will activate its effect on an enemy the turn -AFTER- you click it, so you potentially need to take 2 hits before it removes all enemies, still putting the player in quite a lot of risk if planning to use this stand to beat large groups.
