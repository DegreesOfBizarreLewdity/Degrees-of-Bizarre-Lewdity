Hermit Purple

Hermit is a Skulduggery skill Stand, leaning towards Low submissiveness. Submissiveness ranges from 0 to 850, in other words, a character with the "bratty" or "defiant" trait.

Overview 

Skulduggery skill with low submissiveness Stand. Low raw strength. Great at cheating.

Focuses on hurting/pleasing enemies, and can prevent them from taking actions.


Destructive Power: 1

Speed: 4

Range: 5

Durability: 10

Precision: 6

Ability Potential: 0

Abilities


Passive ability - The spirit can easily cover traces and erase fingerprints, halting crime increase.

Passive ability 2 - The spirit can manipulate any device to grant you success when opening locks, and imitate various sensations to grant you success when stealing. Additionally, allows you to take control over electronic systems and devices.


Main combat ability - Hermit can, right away summon tentacles/vines similar to how enemy tentacles work. It gets up to 10 vines as it progresses, and you can allocate them among multiple targets. Things vines can do are:

  • Sexually pleasure enemies (grab penis etc) - It can then continue pleasuring or squeeze it for damage) Doing this when they are aiming their penis at the PC's orifices, then removes the threat of it penetrating and frees up the PC's body part for use.
  • Stroke (same as player)
  • Slap (Similar to player Punch, with more damage)
  • Grab and restrain enemy arms (Can prevent enemy punching, blocking mouth, stripping the PC and just generally prevents them performing actions with that arm)


Vine Armor (2) - The spirit covers itself around you, reducing all damage you take and damaging aggressors back. This makes use of its S rank durability, greatly reducing the pain you get.


Extra Info

- You can also use the vines against tentacles. It's also supposed to be useful on man-made objects (like the security robots at the compound and the docks (but that hasn't quite been implemented yet))


- Vines can help you cheat (against the cinnamon roll Robin if they're about to beat you while playing video games and also in the arcade, maybe more).


- Due to its ability to restrain multiple hands and sexual organs in a single turn, it is very good at preventing sex and winning fights even without attacking. Or just causing the enemy to be rendered helpless for whatever you want to do to them.
