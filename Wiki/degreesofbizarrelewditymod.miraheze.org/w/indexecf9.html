Echos

Echos is a Dancing skill with high submissiveness Stand. Submissiveness ranges from 1150 to 1500, in other words, a character with the "meek" or "submissive" trait.


Overview 

Dancing skill with high submissiveness Stand. Low raw strength. Used to support its user.

This little fella has 3 forms that are freely changeable at any time (including combat) once unlocked. His abilities have many variables, resulting in an extremely flexible stand.

ACT 2: | Destructive Power: 3 | Speed: 3 | Range: 6 | Durability: 6 | Precision: 5 | Ability potential: 0

ACT 3: | Destructive Power: 6 | Speed: 8 | Range: 3 | Durability: 6 | Precision: 6 | Ability potential: 0


Abilities

ACT 1 ABILITIES

Passive ability: The spirit will help you convince others easier. Plus, Its long range will allow you to be rescued during the night.


Combat Ability: Sound Generation (1) - Creates a sound you can make yourself. The more you repeat the created sounds, the greater their effects. Already made sounds can be created again to multiply its effects.

Sounds & Effects:

  • Please (Plead) - Increases overall trust, plus the effects of the "Plead" speech are greatly increased.
  • Haha (Tease/Mock) - Decreases your trauma, plus the effects of the speeches "Tease" and "Mock" are greatly increased.
  • Shlip (Stroke) - Increases the target's arousal, plus the effects of "Stroke" hand actions are greatly increased.
  • Smooch (Kiss) - Increases the target's arousal, plus the effects of all "Kiss" actions are slightly increased. This includes kissing a penis or a butthole.
  • lik (Lick Breasts//Genitalia) - The effects of all "Lick" actions are increased.
  • Shlurp (Suck Breasts//Genitalia) - Increases the target's arousal, plus the effects of all "Suck" actions are slightly increased. This includes sucking a penis or a nipple.
  • Pow (Punch) - Deals decent damage to the target, plus the damage of "Punch" hand actions is slightly increased.
  • Bam (Kick) - Deals decent damage to the target, plus the damage of "Kick" feet actions is slightly increased.
  • aan (Moan) - The effects of the "Moan" speech are greatly increased.
  • Flap (Stroke/Play genitalia) - The effects of any action that touches one's penis and/or pussy are greatly increased. Won't affect "edging" actions.
  • Crunch (Bite) - Deals decent damage to the target, plus the damage of any "Bite" action is slightly increased.
  • Sorry (Apologise) - Reduces overall anger based on your English skill, plus allows the "Apologise" speech to be used multiple times in the same combat. This ability returns diminishing results.


Out of Combat ability: Mimics a sound While being stalked

Sounds & Effects:

  • Wee-woo - The siren of a police car. Effective inside the town. Decreases the chance of a stalker(s) attacking you.
  • Grrr - The growl of a wolf. Effective in the forest. Decreases the chance of a stalker(s) attacking you.
  • Kee-err - The screech of a hawk. Effective everywhere. Makes a stalker(s) give up on you.


ACT 2 ABILITIES

Combat Ability: Sound Effect - Uses its tail tip to create a physical sound effect. Upon touch, the sound produces its according symbolization. Cost varies for each sound effect. Only one sound effect can be created at a time. Some sound effects are persistant, which means they will stay active even after they trigger. Persistant sound effects can be retrieved in order to create new ones (upon retrieving a sound effect its symbolization will not trigger anymore).

Sounds & Effects:

  • Fwoosh (1) - Creates a strong stream of wind that keeps away most things trying to touch you. Anything already touching you won't be affected. The wind can propel you out of big creatures' mouths. Persistent.
  • Brrrr (1) - Can only be used in machine encounters. Drills any machine limbs that touch you, dealing damage to them. Persistant.
  • Sizzle (2) - Burns anything that touches you, dealing damage. Persistant.
  • Splash (1) - Creates a gush of water that wets anything close by. Removes all bodily fluids in you and lubricates all holes and genitals temporarily. Can cause machines to short circuit, stopping them instantly. Makes you harder to swallow.
  • Boom (3) - Creates a controlled shockwave that sends anything touching you flying. Any body parts being held will be released.
  • Crash (2) - Lauches the tail tip and it explodes on contact, dealing great damage to anything nearby. Won't damage you.


ACT 3 ABILITIES

Three Freeze (1) - Disables one target indefinitely. The spirit becomes unusable while disabling someone. Can be cancelled at any time.

Kerbam bam (2) - A barrage of punches that deals great damage to a single target.


Extra Info

- All 3 forms can also do the basic actions (Punch, Stroke, knock pen etc) however these become unnecessary as Echo has far more (Stackable) potent versions, boosting the PC's basic actions overall resulting in far more gain.

- Amazing support stand, while he focuses one target at a time. He can also use his ACT 2 abilities to target you with an ability/function that will damage or handicap every single enemy targeting you.
